# WS09 Dashboard and Genie

## Objective and Business Outcomes
- Deliver Corporate Finance Actuarial portfolio monitoring dashboards and governed natural-language analytics.
- Improve decision velocity while preserving control, lineage, and trust.

## In Scope
- Dashboard build for movement attribution, trend monitoring, and close readiness.
- Genie space setup on curated and trusted Gold assets.
- Publishing, subscription, and change-control model for reporting assets.

## Out of Scope
- Upstream ingestion and transformation engineering.

## Inputs and Dependencies
- Inputs: WS05 Gold products and WS06 dictionary definitions.
- Dependencies: WS07 quality controls and WS12 access controls.

## Detailed Task Backlog (Sequenced)
1. Define dashboard pages and KPI priority with Corporate Finance Actuarial.
2. Build curated datasets and dashboard visuals.
3. Configure Genie knowledge context with approved definitions and sample questions.
4. Run controlled accuracy and trust checks for Genie responses.
5. Publish dashboards with subscription and release management standards.

## Deliverables
- Corporate Finance Actuarial dashboard pack.
- Genie space with trusted query patterns.
- Consumption and publishing governance playbook.

## RACI-lite
- Accountable: Business analytics owner.
- Responsible: BI engineering and FE solution team.
- Consulted: Corporate Finance Actuarial SMEs and data stewards.
- Informed: finance consumers and executive sponsors.

## Risks and Mitigations
- Risk: KPI definitions drift between dashboards and source tables.
  - Mitigation: dictionary-linked certification for dashboard measures.
- Risk: Genie responses vary for ambiguous phrasing.
  - Mitigation: trusted assets, prompt examples, and benchmark testing.

## Exit Criteria / Acceptance Criteria
- Dashboard UAT signed off.
- Genie quality thresholds met for approved question set.

## Demo Evidence Required for Sign-off
- Demonstrate one KPI drill-down and one validated Genie question path.
