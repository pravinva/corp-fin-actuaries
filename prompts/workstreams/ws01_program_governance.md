# WS01 Program Governance

## Objective and Business Outcomes
- Establish delivery governance that aligns Corporate Finance Actuarial, data platform teams, Field Engineering, and control functions.
- Ensure the program has clear decision rights, escalation paths, and stage-gate sign-off.

## In Scope
- Governance model, meeting cadence, and decision log.
- RAID process and change-control process.
- Program KPIs and reporting format.

## Out of Scope
- Technical implementation details of ingestion, modelling, or dashboards.

## Inputs and Dependencies
- Inputs: sponsor list, stakeholder map, target dates, budget and resource assumptions.
- Dependencies: executive sponsor commitment and named workstream owners.

## Detailed Task Backlog (Sequenced)
1. Confirm sponsor, steering committee, and workstream leads.
2. Define weekly, fortnightly, and monthly governance ceremonies.
3. Stand up RAID and issue escalation path with response SLAs.
4. Define stage-gate checkpoints for each roadmap phase.
5. Publish program KPI scorecard and communication protocol.

## Deliverables
- Governance charter.
- Program calendar and cadence.
- RAID register and change log template usage.
- Stage-gate checklist.

## RACI-lite
- Accountable: Program sponsor.
- Responsible: Program manager.
- Consulted: Corporate Finance Actuarial lead, FE lead, risk and audit.
- Informed: Executive stakeholders and dependent teams.

## Risks and Mitigations
- Risk: Governance forum attendance drops over time.
  - Mitigation: Set mandatory attendance and escalation if quorum is not met.
- Risk: Late decisions delay technical streams.
  - Mitigation: Decision SLA and interim authority delegation.

## Exit Criteria / Acceptance Criteria
- Governance charter approved.
- Named owners for each workstream.
- Decision and escalation model signed off.

## Demo Evidence Required for Sign-off
- Walkthrough of governance dashboard showing KPI, RAID, and stage-gate readiness.
